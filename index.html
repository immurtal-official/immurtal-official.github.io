A node represents an individual point within the distributed network, often a computer, a smartphone, or a server, that stores and distributes the music files and associated data. Each node independently verifies and maintains the integrity of data, including audio files and on-chain copyright certificates, ensuring the resilience, security, and smooth operation of the network.

Smart Contracts are programs on Blockcahin that execute automatically when certain conditions are met, for example when a fan pays to download a song, S.C. can distribute payments immediately and transparently, potentially reducing delays and disputes in payment. These smart contracts will be implemented across the whole infrastructure, empowering storing, publishing, distributing, and streaming experiences.
